# MyProfit Sales Automation Plan

## Purpose

The purpose of this plan is to reduce manual selling and make the MyProfit offer reach potential buyers automatically, while keeping Godfred involved only when a prospect becomes serious or pays.

## Core Offer

Entry offer: £79 Restaurant Profit Diagnostic Report.

Promise: send one month of sales, purchases, wages and overheads, and receive a written 48-hour diagnostic showing whether the restaurant is truly making profit and where margin may be leaking.

## Automation Principle

Do not automate blind spam. Automate a lawful, consent-based sales pathway:

1. Attract the right food business owner.
2. Let the owner self-qualify through the page.
3. Capture the lead.
4. Send automatic follow-up emails.
5. Present the £79 payment link.
6. Notify Godfred only when there is a serious lead or payment.
7. Deliver the diagnostic manually or semi-manually.

## Recommended Funnel

Traffic source -> landing page -> free profit check -> email capture -> automatic follow-up -> £79 payment -> diagnostic intake -> Godfred engagement.

## Minimum Automation Stack

- Landing page: current Hostinger/GitHub site.
- Lead capture: Tally, Google Forms, Brevo form, or HubSpot free form.
- Email automation: Brevo free tier, MailerLite, HubSpot free CRM, or Mailchimp.
- Payment: Stripe Payment Link or PayPal payment link.
- Notification: email notification to Godfred when a lead submits or pays.
- Tracking: Google Sheet or HubSpot CRM.

## Recommended No-Code Setup

### Step 1: Create the £79 Payment Link

Create a Stripe or PayPal payment link called:

MyProfit 48-Hour Restaurant Profit Diagnostic — £79

Redirect after payment to a diagnostic intake form.

### Step 2: Create the Lead Form

Fields:

- Name
- Restaurant name
- Email
- Mobile/WhatsApp
- Business postcode
- Approximate monthly sales band
- Main worry: food cost, wages, rent, suppliers, delivery apps, cash flow, pricing, not sure
- Permission to email follow-up

### Step 3: Create the Diagnostic Intake Form

Fields:

- Monthly sales
- Food and drink purchases
- Wages
- Rent and rates
- Energy
- Delivery platform fees
- Supplier issues
- Menu/pricing concerns
- Upload figures/spreadsheet/screenshots

### Step 4: Create Email Sequence

Email 1: deliver the free profit check result and explain the £79 diagnostic.
Email 2, sent after 1 day: remind them that busy restaurants can still lose money.
Email 3, sent after 3 days: give one example of hidden leakage.
Email 4, sent after 5 days: final invitation to order the £79 diagnostic.

### Step 5: Partner Outreach Automation

Target intermediaries, not random restaurants:

- Restaurant bookkeepers
- Small accountants
- Food wholesalers
- POS installers
- Energy brokers
- Insurance brokers
- Restaurant consultants
- Local business networks

Offer referral commission:

- £20 for each £79 diagnostic sold through their referral.
- 15% of the first three months if the client becomes a monthly support client.

## Semi-Automated Outreach Process

Use a spreadsheet with these columns:

- Business name
- Contact name
- Type
- Email
- Website
- Source
- Message sent date
- Follow-up date
- Reply status
- Outcome

Use Gmail templates or Brevo campaigns for compliant email outreach. Do not scrape and mass-spam without legal basis. Keep messages targeted and relevant.

## When Godfred Should Personally Engage

Only engage when:

- A prospect pays £79.
- A serious prospect asks for a call.
- A referral partner wants to cooperate.
- A restaurant owner sends real figures.
- A monthly support opportunity appears.

## Success Test

Run for 14 days.

Minimum validation targets:

- 20 intermediary contacts
- 100 targeted restaurant/food business page views
- 10 free checks
- 3 paid £79 diagnostics
- 1 serious monthly support conversation

Stop or reposition if there is traffic but no form submission, or form submissions but no willingness to pay.

## Important Warning

The business should not be built around Godfred running around restaurants. It should be built around a simple automated path that lets serious buyers reveal themselves.
